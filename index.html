Custom Software Development

When we build custom software we see craft, not code. Whether Acklen solves a complex problem, creates a custom web application or builds a cross-platform mobile application, the work is strong, durable and beautiful. Like an Amish table, our software is art meant to be used.

Shoddy construction will crumble software like it does furniture or buildings. As a shop of master craftsmen, Acklen adheres to Agile software development principles, including domain-driven design, test-driven development, SOLID and sprints. The process delivers functioning project pieces every two to three weeks. Without technical debt, our software is extensible so adding features or making changes is a short process that does not mean starting from scratch. Again.

Agile Coaching and Mentoring

Acklen Avenue adds experience, expertise and enthusiasm to make your team better. After evaluating your software development process we mentor company developers on core Agile principles. Our approach not only moves projects along, it builds lasting improvements in teamwork and internal mentorship. Coaching business leaders on their roles in an Agile environment to provide engaged and timely feedback reinforces such a culture.

We work with business analysts to bring clarity to your requirements. We work with your project manager to bring clarity to your process. We work with your developers to bring clarity to your code. The bottom line is focused and disciplined progress, which is good for your bottom line..

Near Shore Staffing

Acklen Avenue’s CTO lives part of the year in Honduras, where he has recruited the smartest and most tenacious developers and trained them in core Agile practices. Honduras, which is in the same time zone as Nashville, has Central America’s largest per-capita population of English speakers. The culture and Acklen’s training combine in amazing developers who we treat as family.

For companies that need additional hands but want to avoid offshore developers, near-shore staff augmentation can be the perfect solution. We entrust our stars with select clients, often after Agile coaching gets them on a path that produces results.
